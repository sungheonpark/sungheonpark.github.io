Sungheon Park

I am a research associate in Bio-inspired vision lab at Northwestern university. I was a research scientist at Samsung Advanced Institute of Technology (SAIT) where I am working on the intersection of 3D vision and graphics. Prior to joining SAIT, I completed my PhD at Machine Intelligence and Pattern Analysis Lab, Seoul National University, Korea.

Research

My research interest lies in computer vision and deep learning, with emphasis on 3D pose estimation and reconstruction of dynamic objects and scenes.
